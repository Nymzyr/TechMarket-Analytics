# Business Context

## About TechMarket

**TechMarket** is a fictional e-commerce company specializing in consumer electronics, including laptops, smartphones, home appliances, gaming devices, and accessories.

The company operates through an online marketplace and serves customers across multiple regions. As the business grows, management needs reliable data-driven insights to improve operational efficiency, increase customer satisfaction, and maximize revenue.

This portfolio simulates the work of a Data Analyst within TechMarket using a real-world public e-commerce dataset adapted for educational purposes.

---

# Business Goals

The management team wants to answer several important business questions:

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Who are our most valuable customers?
* Which regions perform best?
* How can customer retention be improved?
* Which factors influence customer satisfaction?
* Can we increase conversion through product page improvements?
* How can future sales be predicted?

---

# Role of the Data Analyst

As a Data Analyst, my responsibilities include:

* collecting and preparing data;
* performing exploratory data analysis;
* identifying trends and business opportunities;
* creating SQL reports;
* validating hypotheses using statistical methods;
* building interactive dashboards;
* forecasting business metrics;
* presenting actionable recommendations to stakeholders.

---

# Available Data

The project is based on a public e-commerce dataset that contains information about:

* customers;
* orders;
* products;
* sellers;
* payments;
* product reviews;
* delivery information;
* geographic locations.

These datasets simulate a real production database of an online retailer.

---

# Project Objectives

The purpose of this portfolio is to demonstrate practical data analytics skills by solving realistic business problems rather than simply analyzing datasets.

Each project in this repository represents a typical task performed by a Data Analyst in an e-commerce company.

The final outcome is a complete analytics workflow, from raw data exploration to business recommendations and decision support.
